About Us

M/S TECH-CON CONSULTING ENGINEERS

We take this opportunity to introduce ourselves,
Tech-Con Consulting Engineers, as one of the upcoming firm of engineering consultants to offer our professional services in the field of architecture, interior designing, structural designing, repairs & restoration and last but not least project management consultancy services for redevelopment works. TCCE comprises of young dynamic engineers who are constantly striving for achieving and maintaining high working standards. All TCCE projects are executed adopting all the latest technologies, correct technical approach under our supervision. In today's competitive environment clients are looking for all the services under one roof, that is what TCCE provides. Our team of associate consultants offers a host of building construction services under one roof. A team of dedicated professionals from their fields are associated with us since the inception of the company.
